About destiny word tv /

Destiny word tv "Timely word for your destiny"

We inspire and encourage our viewers to have a meaningful relationship with Christ and to build up a daily relationship with Jesus.

MISSION

To liberate people to manifest their destinies.

VISION

To see Heaven on Earth and Heaven at Last

Destiny Word Lifestyle

We Trust in God: Our faith, trust, and hope is in Christ. God’s Word guides our decisions, refreshes us, and creates an unshakable faith. Our dependence on God is reflected in our commitment to prayer.

Making Disciples as Jesus commanded

We help you reach others for christ and help them grow and multiply disciples. The goal of the website is to enable the multiplication of disciples.

The power of the Cross

Jesus on the cross, bearing our sins, gives us power to die to sins. Further, we are enabled by the power of the cross of Jesus to live for righteousness

Church participation

We encourage participation in church and offering your time and talents which improve and strengthen both you and the people around you

Trusted by

In our purpose and mission to make it possible for everyone to have a personal encounter with Christ we have been trusted by
